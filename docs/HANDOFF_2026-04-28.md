```
FailureCapture (Aviso) → AI classify → Work Request
                                        ↓
                                       Approve / Express convert (SF-569)
                                        ↓
                                   Work Order (PM01/02/03)
                                        ↓
                              Execution (notif parcial → final auto)
                                        ↓
                                    Closure (firma supervisor)
                                        ↓
                                  Post-Maintenance Review
                                        ↓
                                  RCA (5W+2H, Ishikawa, 5M)
                                        ↓
                              Push to CAPA → ImprovementAction
                                        ↓
                              Push to FMECA (RPN before/after)
                                        ↓
                                Bad Actors → Pareto failure modes
                                        ↓
                            Dashboard executive (Key Insights)
```

Cada flecha está implementada con endpoint y persistencia.
