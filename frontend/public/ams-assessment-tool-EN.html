How to score? (Scale 1 to 5) Click to expand / collapse

▼

For each question, assign a score from 1 to 5 that reflects the CURRENT state of your operation, based on verifiable evidence (documents, CMMS data, audits). Do not score what you think should be in place; score what is actually implemented. If unsure, use the lower level.

Guidelines: (1) Be honest - the value of this diagnostic comes from your objectivity. (2) Answer one phase at a time. (3) Keep evidence notes to back up each score. (4) If a question does not apply to your context, leave it blank.
