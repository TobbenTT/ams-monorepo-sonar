## Core Capabilities

### Work Management
Full lifecycle: **Work Requests → Planning → Scheduling → Execution → Closure**. SAP-integrated workflows with automated status transitions and approval chains.

### AI Agents (CORTEX)
**33 specialized agents**: Equipment Doctor, Predictive Health, Smart Backlog, KPI Watchdog, RCM Advisor, Safety Checklists, Budget Sentinel, Chronic Failure Tracker, and more.

### Analytics & KPIs
Real-time **MTBF, MTTR, OEE, Availability** tracking. Executive and tactical dashboards with trend analysis, Pareto charts, and automated alerts.

### FMEA / RCM
Failure Mode Analysis, RCM decision logic, criticality assessment, FMECA worksheets — aligned with **ISO 14224** standards.

### Smart Scheduling
AI priority-based distribution across Mon-Fri. **Gantt visualization**, HH balance by specialty, material readiness verification before execution.

### Mobile PWA
Field technician app: create WRs, execute tasks, capture photos. **Works offline** with automatic sync when reconnected.
